About Me

Why hello there. My name is Susie. In the past, I've been the City Manager at Instacart, Director of Operations at Hello Fresh, Sales Manager at Morningstar, and root beer float maker at A&W.

In all of my jobs, perhaps with the exception of root beer float maker, I found creative ways to apply data to make business decisions and gain competitive advantages. My favorite part of the day were always data munging, analysis, and general data nerdery. One day in 2015 a light bulb went on and I decided to work with data full-time. I have since graduated from Galvanize's Data Science Immersive program and I'm in the process of finding my first full time Data Science job.

I've worked with Machine Learning algorithms including Linear/Logistic Regression, Gradient Descent, Classification & Regression Trees (Random Forest, GBR, AdaBoost), SVM, NLP, and Recommender Systems. In terms of technical languages, I use Python (incl Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Sci-kit Learn, SciPy, Flask), SQL, MongoDB and bits of Spark and HTML/CSS.

In my spare time, I like doing Data Science projects with personal applications, hiking, and eating cookies.

If you'd like to start a conversation, feel free to email or ping me on linkedin.
